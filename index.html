Investment Thesis

Our Thesis

We believe attractive risk-adjusted returns come from acquiring underperforming assets and applying sound operational practices.

The WCSB offers meaningful opportunities created by consolidation dynamics and capital reallocation from smaller operators.

Our approach is straightforward: we're operators first. Value comes through hands-on improvement, not commodity price speculation.

Operational Value Creation
Returns driven by improvement, not speculation. Downside protection through cost discipline.
Structured Evaluation
Every opportunity goes through a multi-phase due diligence process.
Aligned Interests

Scalable Platform

Acquisition Criteria

What We Look For

Producing Assets

Established production with identifiable upside through optimization, workovers, or infill drilling.

Undervalued Potential

Assets where value hasn't been fully realized due to capital constraints or strategic misalignment.

Western Canada Focus

Alberta and northeastern British Columbia where our networks and expertise run deepest.

Capital Efficient

Reasonable returns with manageable risk and clear paths to cash flow.

Clean Liabilities

Manageable environmental and abandonment profiles with clear compliance paths.

This policy is designed in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Alberta privacy legislation.

  • Contact information such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Professional information including company name, job title, and LinkedIn profile
  • Inquiry details and the content of messages you send through our contact or application forms

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and access times. This data is collected through standard server logs and analytics tools.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about our services
  • To evaluate investment partnerships and facilitate investor communications
  • To process employment applications and assess candidate qualifications
  • To improve our website functionality, content, and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights
  • To send relevant updates about Outwest Energy, where you have opted in to receive them

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating our website and business, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • With professional advisors such as legal counsel, accountants, and auditors as necessary
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or governmental authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Outwest Energy, our users, or the public
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where your information may be transferred as part of that transaction

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit, secure server infrastructure, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations. Contact inquiries are typically retained for two years. Employment application materials are retained for one year following the completion of the hiring process. Investor communications are retained for the duration of the investment relationship plus any period required by securities regulations.

6. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Withdraw your consent for the collection or use of your information, subject to legal or contractual restrictions
  • Request deletion of your personal information where permitted by law
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected].

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytical data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality. We use only essential and analytics cookies — we do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.
